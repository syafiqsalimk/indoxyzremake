THE WORLD'S BEST

Indonesia - the world’s most diversified coffee nation - is synonymous with coffee since the Dutch colonial period of the late 1600s.

The Dutch brought coffee plants to Indonesia to cultivate for export back to Europe during the spice trade period.

Java became the first Indo island to grow coffee and slowly spread to the islands of Sumatra to the west and Toraja to the east.

Today, Indonesia is the world’s fourth largest coffee producer by output and world’s most diversified variety of coffee.
